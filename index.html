UMHackathon

The UMHackathon 2021 is an August-Hackathon organized by Faculty of Computer Science & Information Technology, University of Malaya, focusing on Data Science and Machine Learning, where the participants will need to develop a prototype based on the problem statement given.

LEARN MORE REGISTER NOW

Objectives

  • To promote university students’ awareness and interest about the capabilities and benefits of Microsoft Azure platform.
  • To solve the real-world data challenges related to agriculture, finance and healthcare given by industry experts.
  • To improve university student’s problem-solving abilities.
  • To discover top tier system developers and data scientists among students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I sign up?

Registration link:

Who can participate in the UMHackathon?

UMHackathon is open to undergraduates from public universities in Malaysia.

What are the participation requirements?

To participate, you are required to form a team of 3 to 5 members, with at least one member currently studying at University of Malaya.

How to register?/ When does registration for participants open?

The registration form will be open from 1/7/2021 (12:00 p.m.) until 14/7/2021 (11:59 p.m). Stay tuned on our social media accounts to get the newest information.

Are there any costs for me?

Participants are required to pay RM30 per team as commitment fees. The commitment fees will be returned if your team successfully submits your ideation video and join all the workshops organized by UMHackathon 2021.

When does the UMHackathon take place?

UMHackathon will be held from 2/8/2021 until 17/9/2021. There will be two submission rounds in this period, which are Ideation Round and Prototype Demonstration Round.

For the Ideation Round, all the teams that successfully registered in UMHackathon are eligible to join.
Only the top 14 teams from the Ideation Round are eligible to join for the Prototype Demonstration Round.

How is the UMHackathon conducted?

UMHackathon will be held virtually using the Microsoft Teams platform. There will be a general channel where the opening ceremony or workshops will be held here. Besides, discussion channels will also be available for participants to ask questions related to the field or Azure software during the hackathon.

What will we need to do for the Ideation Round and Prototype Demonstration Round?

For the Ideation Round, participants will need to submit a video presentation to show their ideas on how to solve the given problem statement.

For the Prototype Demonstration round, the shortlisted participants must build a prototype based on their ideation proposed in the first submission by using Microsoft Azure platform. Participants are required to submit their solution that includes source codes and a demonstration video of their prototype or model.

What if I can’t find teammates?

Participants are allowed to register as a team, in pairs or even an individual. We encourage the participants to accept team matching to know more people from other universities and expand your network. Feel free to register with your friend(s) or on your own and we will get teammates for you!

How does the team member matching work?

  • Team members matching will only be provided to teams who are willing to have it and is on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Requests for team changing are not allowed.
  • Participants are encouraged to join an ice-breaking session to get to know their team members.

When do we need to pay the commitment fee?

The commitment fee will be collected after the ice-breaking session. Our committees will send an email to team leaders regarding the payment process and team leaders are required to submit the payment receipt within 3 days.
