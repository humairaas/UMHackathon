UMHackathon 2021 & Objectives

  1. The UMHackathon 2021 is a August-Hackathon organized by Faculty of Computer Science & Information Technology, University of Malaya and Microsoft in collaboration with PEKOM, UOB Asset Management (Malaysia) Berhad and CREST.
  2. The main objectives of UMHackathon 2021 is to promote university students’ awareness and interest about the capabilities and benefits of Microsoft Azure platform and encourage university students to think creatively and critically in solving problems.

Participation Criteria

  1. The UMHackathon is open to all participants (“Participants”); everyone who is at least 18 years of age and pursuing undergraduate study in any public university in Malaysia.
  2. Participants are required to participate in a team of 3 - 5 members with at least one (1) undergraduate student from the University of Malaya.
  3. In the event a team wishes to participate, all the members will be considered Participants however the team shall elect one member to be the Team Leader (“Team Leader”) who will be person-in-contact with UMHackathon 2021 organiser and presented the prize in case of winning with no liability on UMHackathon 2021 Organiser on how such prize will be split between the team members.

Submission

  1. There are a total of two competition rounds for UMHackathon 2021, namely Ideation round and Prototype Demonstration round. Only selected teams will be entitled to compete in Prototype Demonstration round based on the Ideation round submission.
  2. Each team may submit only one entry for each competition round. The teams have time to work and finalize their entry and are required to submit before the announced deadline stated for each competition round.
  3. In Ideation Round, Participants are required to submit a proposal video which shows the team's ideas on how to solve the given problem statement.
  4. In Prototype Demonstration round, Participants should demonstrate a working prototype and submit the prototype demonstration video along with source code. The cost of the prototype shall be borne by the participants if needed.
  5. All the video submissions for both Ideation Round and Prototype Demonstration Round must be in .mp4 format and the video length should be not more than 10 minutes. Participants are required to upload the video in Google Drive or Youtube and drop the link in the online submission form provided by the UMHackathon 2021 Organiser respectively. For those who uploaded the video on Youtube must change the video privacy settings to private.
  6. Participants shall ensure that the team information and video provided upon submission online is true and accurate with no liability on UMHackathon 2021 in case of its inaccuracy. No late submissions will be accepted and any incomplete, false, erroneous, fraudulent, forged, tampered forms will not be considered.
  7. Once the submission is done, the Team Leader shall receive a confirmation email from UMHackathon 2021 one week or less after the submission date.

Judging Criteria

The Judging Panel and the relevant selection criteria for the decision will be announced during the briefing session.
  2. Selected team to emerge in Final Submission round will be announced in UMHackathon 2021 website as well as in any publicity posts or in any form of media. Participants shall check on the UMHackathon 2021 regularly for updates on each announcement.
